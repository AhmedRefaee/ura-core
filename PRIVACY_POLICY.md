# Privacy Policy for URA

_Last updated: 2026-06-25_

URA ("the app") is an internal operations tool for managing inventory, orders, and team communication. This policy explains what data the app collects and how it's used.

## Data We Collect

- **Account information**: email address and password (stored securely by Supabase Auth; we never see or store your raw password).
- **Profile information**: name, role, and any profile details you enter.
- **Chat data**: messages and images you send within the app's chat feature.
- **Inventory and order data**: items, quantities, and files (e.g. Excel imports) you create or upload while using the app.
- **Push notification tokens**: a device token used to deliver notifications via Firebase Cloud Messaging. We do not collect device location.

## How We Use Your Data

Data is used solely to operate the app's core features: authentication, chat between team members, inventory/order tracking, and push notifications. We do not sell or share your data with advertisers.

## Third-Party Services

- **Supabase** — hosts our database, authentication, and file storage.
- **Firebase Cloud Messaging** — delivers push notifications.

Both providers process data only as needed to provide these services and are bound by their own privacy/security commitments.

## Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. To request deletion of your account and associated data, contact us at the email below.
